Zachary Ragent

Violinist and Educator

Mr. Ragent is a violinist with the New World Symphony in Miami Beach, FL and the Peninsula Music Festival in Door County, WI. He studied at the University of Michigan, where he received both a Master's and Specialist's degree in violin performance, and did his undergraduate work at the University of California at Santa Cruz, also in violn performance.
